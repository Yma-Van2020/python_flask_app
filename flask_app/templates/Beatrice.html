Meet Beatrice

Majestic Beatrics is looking for a family she can retire with. Are you part of her retirement plan? Beatrics came into our care as a stray, and although we don’t know her history, we do know how much her craves a family to call her own. Beatrics is a sweet, loving cat who would love to tell you all of her tales and adventures, while he happily drools on your lap. Once comfortable, which doesn’t seem to take very long, she will lovingly greet you with a head boop. Beatrics does tend to be overstimulated by too much physical attention, so his family will need to be able to tell when she’s had enough attention…for now. That being said, she would do best in a home that is able to read her body language and with no young children. While in shelter, Beatrics has shown how much she needs to be the king of her own castle! Beatrics would do best in a home as the only animal, to reign over her kingdom. Sometimes Beatrics doesn’t always hear us coming, so we do believe she may be deaf. That being said, Beatrics will need to be an indoor cat to keep her safe. Do you have room in your heart and home for this gentle soul? An adoption counseling session with our Adoption Staff is necessary before adopting any animal.
